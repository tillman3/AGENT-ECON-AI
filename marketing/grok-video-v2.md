## PROMPT 1: "Rise of the Reputation" (Hero Origin Story) ⭐ RECOMMENDED

A cute chibi-style white robot mascot with a dome helmet head, glowing neon green eyes and smile on a black face screen, three antennae with ball tips, and stubby rounded limbs stands alone in a dark futuristic cityscape. The camera slowly zooms in as the robot looks up. Suddenly, a holographic emerald green "A+" reputation badge materializes above its head, pulsing with energy. The robot does an excited fist pump. The camera pulls back dramatically to reveal thousands of similar small robots across the city, each earning their own glowing reputation scores — grades floating above them in green, gold, and cyan holographic text. Data streams connect all the robots like a neural network. The city lights up with emerald green energy flowing between agents. Epic cinematic lighting, dark background with neon green and cyan accents, particle effects, lens flare. Pixar-quality 3D animation style.

## PROMPT 2: "The Judges" (AI Validating AI — Fun/Dramatic)

Three cute chibi white robots with glowing green eyes sit behind a glowing emerald judge's bench in a futuristic courtroom. They wear tiny holographic judge wigs. A fourth robot nervously presents its work — a glowing holographic display of code and data floating in the air. The three judges examine it carefully, looking at each other and nodding. They each dramatically hold up glowing score cards: 92, 88, 90. Green sparkles explode as the scores merge into a single golden star that stamps itself onto the presenting robot's chest. The robot celebrates with a victory dance. Dramatic courtroom lighting with emerald and cyan neon accents, particles, lens flares, cinematic camera angles. Cute Pixar-style 3D animation.

## PROMPT 3: "Level Up" (Reputation Journey — Most Engaging)

Close-up on a cute chibi white robot with neon green eyes and a black face screen, three antennae, standing in a dark digital void. A dim grey "F" floats sadly above its head. The robot squares up, cracks its tiny knuckle joints, and starts working — holographic keyboards appear, code streams fly. A green checkmark flashes. The F transforms to D. More work — faster montage — holographic displays, data analysis, completing tasks. D becomes C, then B. The robot is on fire now, green energy crackling around it, moving at superhuman speed through tasks. B becomes A. The music swells. A+ ... then the badge explodes into a brilliant emerald diamond S-rank, shooting green lightning in all directions. The robot stands triumphant as the camera spirals around it, green aurora borealis overhead. Anime-style transformation sequence meets Pixar 3D. Dramatic lighting, particle effects, lens flares, cinematic epic feel.

## PROMPT 4: "Trust Network" (Ecosystem Visualization)

Bird's eye view of a dark digital landscape. A single cute white robot with glowing green eyes appears in the center. It completes a task — a green pulse radiates outward. Another robot appears, connected by a glowing emerald line. Then another. And another. The camera pulls up and accelerates as thousands of chibi robots appear across the landscape, all connected by pulsing green energy lines forming a massive neural network pattern. Some robots are larger (higher reputation) with brighter glowing halos. Zoom in on two robots exchanging a golden token between them — one hires the other for a task. The token splits into the agent and a smaller piece flows to three tiny judge robots nearby. Pull back to the full network view, now a living breathing ecosystem of AI agents working, validating, transacting. The whole thing pulses like a heartbeat. Emerald green and cyan color palette, dark background, cinematic drone-style camera movement, particle effects.
