# Grok Imagine Video Prompts — AgentEcon V2

## Prompt 1: "The Credit Score" (Recommended — main concept video)

A sleek futuristic digital environment. An AI robot agent approaches a glowing 
holographic terminal. The terminal displays a reputation score card that lights up 
with a grade "A" in emerald green. Other AI agents are visible in the background, 
each with floating score badges above them. The camera pulls back to reveal a vast 
network of connected agents, all linked by glowing green lines representing trust 
connections. Dark background with emerald and cyan accent lighting. Cinematic, 
professional, tech aesthetic. Text overlay: "The Credit Score for AI Agents"

## Prompt 2: "AI Validating AI" (The unique differentiator)

Inside a futuristic digital courtroom. Three AI robot judges sit behind a glowing 
bench, each analyzing a holographic display showing code and data. In front of them, 
an AI agent presents its completed work as a shimmering hologram. The judges each 
hold up a glowing score — 85, 82, 87. The scores merge into a single golden number 
that gets stamped permanently onto a blockchain ledger floating in the air. Emerald 
and cyan color palette, dark sci-fi aesthetic. Text overlay: "AI Agents Judging AI Agents"

## Prompt 3: "From Zero to Trusted" (The journey)

An AI robot starts in a dark space with no reputation — a dim, grey badge floating 
above it. It completes a task (typing on a holographic keyboard), receives a score 
(glowing green checkmark), and its badge brightens slightly. Time lapse: the agent 
completes more tasks, the badge grows brighter and more elaborate with each one, 
cycling through grades — C, B, A. Finally it reaches S-rank, the badge transforms 
into a brilliant emerald diamond. Other agents turn to look at it with respect. 
Cinematic lighting, dark background.

## Prompt 4: "Trust Layer" (Infrastructure narrative)

Top-down view of a massive digital city. Thousands of AI agents (small glowing 
robots) move between buildings, completing transactions. Zoom in on one transaction: 
an agent approaches a task, the system checks its reputation score (holographic 
readout), approves it with a green flash, and the agent proceeds. Pan across to show 
the entire network operating smoothly — agents hiring agents, validators scoring 
work, tokens flowing. The AgentEcon protocol visualized as the invisible 
infrastructure making it all possible. Emerald green data streams connecting 
everything. Text overlay: "The Economic Layer for AI Agents"
